  • Kay Sun, Ph.D.
    Updated December 1, 2023

    Philosophy

    My philosophy towards end-to-end, scalable data and AI that is ready for production consists of,

    • Proper data management and governance with orchestrated pipelines to ensure data quality and minimize manual processes and interventions.
    • Democratize data by making data accessible, employing role-based permissions and data masking to ensure data protection and privacy.
    • Lay a solid foundation in data operations to facilitate eventual data science/machine learning operations (MLOps) by standardizing, automating, and orchestrating repeatable processes for scalable deployment.
